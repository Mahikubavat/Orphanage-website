Why Choose Us?

Providing Shelter and Basic Needs: Ensuring children have a safe place to live, access to food, clean water, clothing, and medical care.

Emotional Support: Offering a loving, stable environment where children can form secure attachments with caregivers. This is crucial for their emotional development and well-being.

Education: Enabling children to attend school and providing them with educational resources to help them succeed academically. Some orphanages may offer additional educational programs such as vocational training or special needs support.

Healthcare: Ensuring that children have access to regular medical check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment for any health issues. Orphanages often work with local health organizations to provide comprehensive care.

Life Skills: Teaching children essential skills, such as hygiene, cooking, financial literacy, and communication, to prepare them for independent living when they reach adulthood.

Social Integration and Advocacy: Supporting children’s emotional growth by helping them form relationships with other children and adults. Many orphanages also advocate for children’s rights and work to find adoptive families or reunite children with their biological families if possible.

Our Mission

Rights Protection: Advocate for the children’s legal rights, ensuring they are treated fairly and are not exploited.

Family Reunification: In cases where possible, work with social services to reunite children with their biological families or help find suitable adoptive families.

Achivements

Mr.sharma

Founder Of Balashram

It is with great joy and gratitude that I stand before you today to share the success of our beloved orphanage, Nayi Umeed. When we first began this journey, we had little more than a dream—a dream to provide a safe, loving home for children who had lost everything. Today, we see the fruits of that dream blossoming in the success stories of each child that has passed through our doors. From the moment we opened, the children who came to Nayi Umeed were given more than just food and shelter—they were given love, care, and the opportunity to thrive. With the support of our dedicated staff, volunteers, and generous donors, we have seen these children grow into confident, capable individuals who are achieving their dreams and making a difference in the world.

The success of an orphanage is ultimately defined by the transformation of the children in its care. When children leave the orphanage empowered, educated, emotionally stable, and ready to contribute to society, that is the true measure of success. It reflects the hard work, dedication, and love invested by the staff, volunteers, and supporters, and it speaks to the lasting legacy of an institution committed to creating better futures for vulnerable children.

At Balashram providing a safe, nurturing, and supportive environment for children who have lost their families, or who have been abandoned. Every day, we work to offer them not just shelter, but the love, care, and guidance they need to heal from past hardships and to build a future full of hope and possibility.

Our children come to us with unique stories, each marked by loss and adversity. But within the walls of Balashram, they find warmth, stability, and the opportunity to dream once again. With access to education, emotional support, and guidance, we are helping these children build the skills and confidence needed to become successful, self-sufficient adults.

However, we cannot do this alone. The journey of these children requires the support of caring individuals like you—whether through donations, volunteering, or simply spreading awareness about our mission. Your generosity makes it possible for us to continue providing a place where children feel loved, valued, and encouraged to thrive.

Together, we can help transform their lives and provide them with a future full of opportunities. Every bit of support counts and makes a profound difference.

Thank you for being part of this journey and for helping us make the world a better place for these children.
